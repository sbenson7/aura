Welcome to AURA

AURA salt cave and wellness offers services unique to the alternative health and wellness community. Our focus is on all natural and holistic services for overall well-being of mind, body and spirit. Our mission is to offer a natural, holistic and calm environment where treatments are used to create a safe, drug-free alternative to provide relief and improve overall wellness for all ages.

We opened our doors in East Amherst, NY in July 2017 and our wellness center is truly one of a kind. In addition to our Healing Salt Cave® and The Halo Room, we offer hand/foot detox, infrared saunas, foot soaks, oxygen bar, aromatherapy, massage, reflexology, reiki, chakra balancing, hypnosis, mindful living and other holistic services. We offer individual sessions as well as various classes and events.
